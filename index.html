About

Fran

Originally trained in Beauty and Hairdressing before working in the fitness industry as a Group Exercise Instructor for more than 30 years, her dream of running her own business became a reality when she opened Green Serene in her 18th-century Cottage home.

Fran's extensive qualifications include Diplomas in L3 & L4 Sports Massage Therapy, Swedish and Hot Stone Massage, Luxury Bespoke Facials, Waxing as well as being Emergency First Aid at Work qualified.

Ethos

Fran's core principles are rooted in a profound commitment to nurturing beauty and wellness, naturally.

Advocating for sustainable practices as well as organic, vegan and cruelty-free products reflects her commitment to your skins health and the well-being of our planet.
